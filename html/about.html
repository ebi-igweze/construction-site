    • Masonry Building tomorrow, today.
    Established in 2012, Masonry has been a construtor to major multi-national house builders. Our commitment to innovation sets us apart from all. From our employees to clients, subcontractors, partners, and our considerable network of contacts, building strong, resilient relationships is the key to our success.
    Why Choose Masonry We hire the best and then give our people the freedom to decide how they accomplish their work and make our clients and each other successful. We want our employees to have the confidence to express and apply their unique innovations and expertise. We also believe in partnering collaboratively with our clients so together we can innovate beyond the boundaries traditionally associated with construction.
    Unrivalled Expertise Our experienced and knowledgeable employees are the reason Masonry is consistently ranked number one in several markets across Canada and internationally. We empower people, not processes, and deliver on our commitments to each other, which makes us extremely fast, fluid, and flexible. Mutual accountability at Masonry runs in every direction – down, up and sideways.
    Sustainability At Masonry, sustainability isn’t just something we talk about; it’s something we simply do every single day (and have for years). Sustainability is vital to how we conduct ourselves as we embark on building the future. It is a mission that connects our employees and clients alike.
    Community Consciousness At the heart of Masonry is a deep commitment to the communities we operate in. Masonry is dedicated to a comprehensive and collaborative approach to Indigenous engagement: Acting as a responsible and respectful business partner, Working side by side with community leaders and members, Creating and nurturing meaningful and beneficial opportunities and relationships.
    Safety We believe everyone should go home safe to their families every night. As a company that believes in safety not as a matter of business but of integrity, Masonry is committed to developing a strong safety culture, eliminating injuries, and protecting and maintaining the complete well-being of each employee as well as the health and safety of the communities we work in.
